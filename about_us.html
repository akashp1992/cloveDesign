Founder Story

A personal crisis and subsequent recovery in 2019 made Niraj Doshi realise the gaps around mental health in Asia societies.

So, after a 15 year Corporate stint abroad, he returned to India to launch SAL.

The SAL mission was built around 3 Es

-Educate(Mental health awereness),

-Eradicate(stigmas & myths) and

-Elevate(emotional well-being)

Today, as SAL grows its philanthropic efforts, it had branched further, adding Clove as a proprietary solution suite.

Through SAL and Clove, the mission is still the same today-provide afforadable, accessible and effective mental health services to create a stigma-free, emotionally uplifted society.

Vision

To eradicate suicides in Asia by 2030 through access to quality mental health support

Mission

To help people anyone who needs help get access to it through Clove's innovative and reliable platform

Values

Accessbility-We believe emotional wellbieng is a basic human right and our platform should be available and affordable for all

Trust-We act transparency, respect & confidentiality for clients, colleagues and partner alike

Oppennes-We invite Individuals, communities and organisations to collectively help make wold an emotionally healthier place
